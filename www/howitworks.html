Qualitech compliance solution meets companies' needs for reporting, monitoring, tracking, and enhancing social performance. A comprehensive, integrated web-based solution, Qualitech compliance solution offers the following functionalities:

 

 

 

  • Data collection

 

  • Consolidation

 

  • Reporting

 

  • Management

 

Qualitech compliance solution is more than just software. At the core of our solution are intelligent Data Services Hubs that manage data capture and extraction from across the globe. Whether you are capturing information from smart meters, supplier reports, internal business systems or simply from paper based documents, data can be securely processed within minutes and made available online via a web browser.

 

 

 

Our Compliance Service

 

Compliance Audits could be arranged within 3 business days notice, and reports are issued within 48 hours after the factory audit. Standard text reports are with photos illustration for clear reference. Our well trained and experienced auditors would also give remarks about the factory for more all-rounded reference. To answer customers' specific concerns, we could also customize audit check points. By checking on the company organization, factory facilities and systems, operational procedures, labor working conditions, and any aspects involved in the production process, you will be ensured that the factory you have chosen do have the capability to produce your products with a consistent quality level and comply with Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).

 

 

 

 

Benchmarking of your data

 

Buyer can monitor the performance of their auditee in terms of "score" under different criteria. To demonstrate the risk of compliance, buyer can alter the parameters such as area, audit date, industry & type of enterprise. These charts allow the buyer to monitor and track the performance of their selected auditee over a period of time.

Compliance grading analysis tool allow the buyer to determine the number of violated legal regulations of the selected auditee group. This tool also allow the buyer to determine the average score of the chosen management system criteria under a specific period.

 

Supplier Compliance Ratio tool allows the buyer to analysis the number of non-conformances per year over a chosen period of time. To determine the non-compliance ratio % of each legal regulation, hence identify the area of the majority of their risk of their selected group of auditee. The buyer can identify the compliance ratio % of their selected groups of auditee for each management criteria.
