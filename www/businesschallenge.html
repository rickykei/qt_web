Because business activities are becoming increasingly international, and because many countries have increased and tightened their social regulations, a growing number of companies are reporting on their social performance. As a result, companies have a variety of new needs in terms of information and performance management:

 

 

 

  • Social reporting (official Social Report, CoC report, etc.)
  • Compliance monitoring (compliance with local social laws, application of companies' internal codes of conduct)
  • Management of human capital, a key factor for competitiveness

 

In order to successfully meet these challenges, companies must have the right reporting tools. HR information systems are not flexible enough and can only partially meet the social reporting needs of human resources and other operational departments.

 

The Qualitech compliance solution has been designed specifically to meet these new needs: it allows companies to simplify all of their reporting and social monitoring processes and make them more dependable. 

 

 

 

Rapid Business Environment Changes Affecting Risk Management

 

Globalization, worldwide supply chains, new environmental, social and ethical standards, fears about product safety, consumers and NGOs' pressure: companies now live in an environment where supply chain management is both increasingly challenging and decisive in terms of business performance and risk prevention.

 

Corporate responsibility is now extending beyond the company's boundaries to include the global supply chain. The "supplier risk" threatens at any time companies' brands, reputation, security and business continuity.

 

Without efficient and reliable systems to measure, manage and improve sustainability performance at all levels of the supply chain, companies are at risk and unable to react to crisis.

Essential determinants of a company's performance are the challenges which relate to sustainable development.


Non-financial criteria are now used by stakeholders (employees, investors, customers, suppliers, rating agencies and the media...) to analyze and compare companies.

 

The requirements for sustainable development data have become progressively more demanding in terms of their degree of transparency, reliability and their ability to be audited. 

This being so, a company's capacity to manage and report on their sustainable development performance plays an integral role in the management of their risk and enhancement of operational performance.
