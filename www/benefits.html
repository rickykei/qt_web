Simplified reporting and lower costs

 

Social reporting processes take up a company's time, resources and energy, both in human resources and in the company's different functional areas. Qualitech compliance solution offers the following benefits:

 

 

 

 

      • Simplified data collection
      • Lower reporting costs
      • Automated production of social reports
      • Allows teams to concentrate on tasks with higher added value

 

 

Through its administration modules, the Qualitech solution enables companies to automate data collection and consolidation processes, thereby reducing the human and financial costs of reporting.

 

 

 

More reliable performance data

 

 

Social reporting is a complex exercise. Data is not homogeneous, and it is often difficult to define indicators consistently throughout a company's many and often varied sites and activities.

 

Qualitech compliance solution allows for more reliable social data through:

 

 

  • Consistency tests
  • Automated unit conversion functionalities
  • Integrated data input validation workflow
  • Audit functions, which facilitate data verification

 

 

 

 

Improvement in management and performance

 

Qualitech compliance solution allows you to analyze and fine-tune a company's social performance beyond statutory reporting requirements. Social compliance experts use Qualitech compliance solution for reporting on the following in particular:

 

 

 
  • Employees
  • Age groups
  • Gender diversity
  • Number of managerial and non-managerial staff recruitments and departures
  • Absenteeism, including that which is due to industrial accidents
  • Training
  • Transfers
  • Rating of workplace atmosphere

 

 

Qualitech compliance solution enables companies to focus on monitoring through:

 

 
  • Identification and sharing of best practices

  • Measurement of compliance with social regulation
  • Improvement of social performance
  • Identification and reduction of social risks
  • Thorough and in-depth review of social performance
  • Easier monitoring at corporate, business unit, and facility levels
